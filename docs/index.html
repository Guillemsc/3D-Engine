Biography

AFK is a company created by five students from the UPC as part of the subject Project II.

The requeriments for this project was to make a new game from scratch using assets from Age of Empires or the Legend of Zelda a Link to the Past

The idea of the game was born thanks to our love for Role Playing Games, so all the team agreed to develop a story-based Age of Empires and started to create the world of Khanelyf

Project

Age of Empires: The Lost Lands of Khanelyf is a RTRPG videogame made by AwayFromKeyboard. The game follows the story of a hero in the lands of Khanelyf, he will have to go through challenges to conquer the land.

A rpg that reminds us of Warcraft III but giving more value to the strategy and troop movement of Age of Empires and also keeping their art style. The game supports tutorials and the singleplayer mode. Every acomplishment you make will elevate your status and gain you respect from your people allowing you to make your civilitzation stronger.
